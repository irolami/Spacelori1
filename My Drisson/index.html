My Drisson

My Drisson, oh how fair and bright,

A beauty that shines both day and night,

With eyes that sparkle like the stars above,

And a smile that fills my heart with love,

.

Your voice is like a gentle breeze,

That soothes my soul with such ease,

Your laughter, like the sweetest song,

That fills my heart and makes it strong,

.

But alas, I know our time is brief,

For life is like a falling leaf,

And though we may part one day,

Your memory in my heart will stay,.

.

My Drisson, my love for you will never fade,

For in my heart, it will always be engraved,

And though we may be worlds apart,

You will always occupy a special part.
