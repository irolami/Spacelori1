My Nepenthe

My Nepenthe, my sweet escape,

A haven from the world's harsh scrape,

Through troubles and trials I do trudge,

But in your embrace, I find my refuge.

.

Your gentle touch, your soothing balm,

Eases my soul, brings me calm,

As I rest in your tranquil embrace,

All my worries seem to erase.

.

In your presence, I find my peace,

A respite from life's endless lease,

And though the world may spin and turn,

In your embrace, I will always yearn.

.

My Nepenthe, my sanctuary,

A place where I can always tarry,

In your loving arms, I find my rest,

And in your heart, I am forever blessed.
