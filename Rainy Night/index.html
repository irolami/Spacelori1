Rainy Night

You remind me of rain, gentle and light,

Falling from above, a peaceful sight.

Your presence brings calm to my mind,

And washes away the worries I find.

.

Just like the rain, you nourish the earth,

Bringing new life and a sense of rebirth.

Your kindness and warmth, like the sun's rays,

Illuminate my life in countless ways.

.

So when I see raindrops falling from the sky,

I think of you and the joy you supply.

Thank you for being a constant source of peace,

Your love and care will never cease.
